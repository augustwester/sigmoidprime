About sigmoid prime

The best way to learn a new concept is to try and explain it in simple, intuitive terms.

When you are new to machine learning, forcing yourself to thoroughly articulate a challenging concept is an efficient way of moving from understanding it to getting it. The purpose of sigmoid prime is to provide myself with a channel to do just this. If you are new to the field (like I am), hopefully this will turn both of us into better machine learning practitioners.
